History

Where I've been — and why it matters.

My Journey

Early Years

Roots in Toledo, Ohio

Born and raised in Toledo, Ohio, Tonya grew up navigating a life marked by hardship, instability, and a deep longing to belong. Her early years were defined by resilience — learning to survive circumstances that would have broken many, and finding strength in the very challenges that tried to hold her back.

High School

Libbey High School

Tonya attended Libbey High School in Toledo, where she began to discover her voice, her love of sports, and her ability to lead. The community she found there planted the seeds for a lifelong commitment to youth development and coaching.

Coaching Career

Building the Next Generation

Tonya stepped into coaching as more than a role — it became a calling. Working with youth across Toledo, she built programs that combined athletic development with life skills, mentorship, and character-building. Her teams didn't just compete; they grew into leaders.

Community Work

Serving Toledo & Beyond

Beyond the field, Tonya has dedicated herself to community impact — organizing events, advocating for equity and access, and creating spaces where people who are overlooked find belonging. Her work has touched hundreds of families in the Toledo area.

Author

"UNLOVED" — Published

Tonya channeled her life story into her debut book, UNLOVED: A Blueprint for Healing the Child You Used to Be. The book became a testament to her journey — proof that no matter where you start, healing and transformation are always possible.

Today

Life Happens Coaching

Through Life Happens Coaching, Tonya now operates at the intersection of coaching, speaking, mentorship, and healing. She works with individuals, youth programs, and organizations to drive real, lasting change — one story at a time.
